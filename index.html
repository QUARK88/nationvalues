NationValues

What is NationValues ?

NationValues is one of the 4 tests of the AllValues series. Its goal is to class national beliefs in a precise and simple manner. Base idea by u/rayz9989.

What are the 14 values ?

There are 7 independent axes - Authority, Restriction, Enforcement, Documents, Populism, Justice and Change - and each has two opposing values assigned to them :
